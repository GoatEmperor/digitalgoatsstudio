1. Homepage:
When you first access the wiki, you'll typically land on the homepage. This is your starting point for exploring the content.
2. Right Sidebar:
The right sidebar is your primary navigation tool. It contains various sections and options to help you find information quickly and easily.
3. Keys:
When on a page click on the sidebar and press Backspace. This will send you back page in your history. An another key that can be used is ~. This key will bring up a menu to report the curret wiki page for Inaccuracy or Misinformation, Outdated Content, Formatting or Style Issues. You can copy a page's link by pressing F4.
