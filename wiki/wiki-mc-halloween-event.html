The Halloween Event happens on October, 31 . The event lasts for one day. The Halloween Event doesn't have any stands.

During the Halloween Event the sky will turn dark, ghost spawn, and jack-o-lanterns are placed all over the map. The bank floats above the ground, but is still rotated. The cave during the Halloween Event is blocked by a jack-o-lantern and can't be entered.
