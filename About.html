At Digital Goats Studio, our passion is crafting entertaining games that cater to a diverse audience of all ages. We take pride in creating
enjoyable experiences that resonate with gamers across the spectrum. Among our diverse portfolio, two standout titles that exemplify our commitment
to fun and innovation are 'Moo Cowboy' and 'The Fight Club.'
Moo Cowboy is game about a Cowboy called Moo who fights brainwashed cow. Aliens ruled by the farther Mayor McCheese / Lord McCheese brainwash these cow to help take over the world.
The aliens fly giant flying sombreros. Moo travels time with his friend, Dr. Dave Pie to stop these invaders.
The Fight Club, the ultimate battleground where players unleash their combat skills and engage in intense battles. In this unique and thrilling ROBLOX game,
you'll experience the adrenaline rush of fighting while also navigating a twisted economy where you can sell your limbs for profit. Prepare yourself for a
one-of-a-kind adventure that combines brutal combat with entrepreneurial instincts.
